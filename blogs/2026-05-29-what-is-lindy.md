I've always wanted a personal assistant that could handle my day-to-day annoyances; unfortunately, I'm not in the right tax bracket. If you feel the same, you'll be happy to know that there's a glimmer of hope: Lindy. It's an AI personal assistant built around email, meetings, and the kind of admin work you'd normally hand off to a human.
