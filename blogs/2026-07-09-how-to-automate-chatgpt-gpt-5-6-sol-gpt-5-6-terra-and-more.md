In a livestream on July 9, OpenAI rolled out the red carpet for not one but three new models: Sol, Terra, and Luna. These celestial models mark three tiers within the GPT-5.6 generation, each built to cut down on how often you have to re-explain your context in ChatGPT. All three are available on Zapier, so you can connect them securely to the other apps you already use in Zap workflows (what we call automations).
