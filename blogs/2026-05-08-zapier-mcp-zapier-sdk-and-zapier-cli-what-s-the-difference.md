If you've been paying attention to new products in the tech space, you may have noticed three initialisms popping up a lot: MCP, SDK, and CLI. Zapier has dedicated products for each: Zapier MCP, Zapier SDK, and Zapier CLI. All three connect AI to Zapier's ecosystem of more than 9,000 apps, and they all run on the same governed access layer.
