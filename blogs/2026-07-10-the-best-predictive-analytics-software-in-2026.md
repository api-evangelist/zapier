You could argue that pretty much all analytics are meant to be predictive. Isn't the point of analyzing past performance, on some level, to project future performance? (I guess you could just be nostalgic for the metrics underlying your favorite past fiscal quarter.) As a dedicated tool class, however, predictive analytics software helps analysts of all kinds see what past data says about the future.
