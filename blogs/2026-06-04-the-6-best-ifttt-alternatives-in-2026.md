A comparison of six automation platforms that offer alternatives to IFTTT, evaluated for power users and business teams who have outgrown IFTTT's simple applet model. Tools reviewed include Zapier, Make, n8n, Microsoft Power Automate, and others, assessed on multi-step workflows, app coverage, pricing, and ease of use. The guide helps readers choose the right platform based on technical skill level and automation complexity requirements.
