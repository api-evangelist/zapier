Ten practical task automation ideas for individuals and teams using Zapier, covering scenarios from auto-creating tasks from emails and Slack messages to syncing to-dos across multiple project management apps. Each idea includes a suggested Zap structure and the apps involved. The post is aimed at productivity-focused users who want to reduce the overhead of manually managing their task lists.
