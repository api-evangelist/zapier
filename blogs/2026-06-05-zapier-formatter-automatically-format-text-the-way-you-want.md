Zapier Formatter is a built-in Zap step that transforms text, numbers, and dates without requiring custom code or external tools. The guide covers how to use Formatter to capitalize names, reformat phone numbers, extract substrings, and standardize date formats across automated workflows. It is one of the most versatile utilities in the Zapier platform for keeping data clean as it moves between apps.
