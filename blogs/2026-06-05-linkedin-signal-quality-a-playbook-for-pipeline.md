This playbook covers how to use LinkedIn engagement signals — profile views, post reactions, connection requests — to identify and prioritize high-quality pipeline opportunities. It walks through strategies for capturing these signals and routing them into CRM systems automatically using Zapier workflows. The guide is aimed at sales and marketing teams looking to improve lead quality from LinkedIn activity.
