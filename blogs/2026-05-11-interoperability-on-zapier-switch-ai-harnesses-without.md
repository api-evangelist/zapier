The AI tool you or your team uses right now probably won't be the same in a year, and that's if we're being conservative. At the pace AI is moving, you might go through a tool migration every few weeks now. It's great to keep up with updates, but these migrations can come with a tax: you've got to reconnect your apps, rewrite your agent instructions, and rebuild your governance guardrails from scratch every time.
